POWERS AND ABILITIES
  • Parkour and Agility:
    Cap is a master of parkour. He can jump many feet in the air and perform flips and somersaults mid-air.
  • Enhanced Physical Condition:
    The Super-Soldier Serum grants him Enhanced Strength, Speed, Reflexes, Durability, Senses and a healing factor. He is capable of coming back alive after death, quickly recover from wounds, can't get drunk and can heal from deadly poisons.
  • Telepathic Defenses:
    Quentin Quire was unable to break into Cap's mind.
  • Genius Level Intellect:
    The Super-Soldier Serum rises his mental capabilities. He has didactic memory.
  • Bravery and Willpower:
    He himself says he'll never back down from a fight. He was capable of breaking mind control and values the lives of others meaning he will do anything to protect them.
  • Leadership:
    Rogers is a born leader and ended up as the leader of the Avengers.
  • Excellent Strategist:
    Steven states that he has learned military tactics from thousands of years of warfare.
  • Stealth:
    Cap was capable of infiltrating a HYDRA base and even got the drop on Crossbones during their duel.
  • Adept in Virtually any Weapon:
    Cap is capable of mastering any weapon in seconds upon taking it into his hands.
  • Master Martial Artist:
    Cap's training of the martial arts is extreme and one of the best in the Marvel Universe.
