Synopsis

Avengers: Endgame was said to be the continuos episode of the movie Avengers: Infinity War. Previously, in Avengers: Infinity War, over half of the characters were destroyed by the "Thanos' snap". Therefore, the storyline started with Avengers killing Thanos. Later, they found out that the Infinity Stones, were destroyed by Thanos and they had to travel to the past to bring the stones back. The storyline continues by Antman who provides the concept of the quantum mechanics while Iron Man creates a time machine. With the creation of time machine, the Avengers Split up to travel back to the key moments throughout the MCU and collect the different Infinity Stones.

Synopsis

The Avengers collected the Infinity Stones back to the present. Then, the Avengers created The Infinity Gauntlet. Hulk brought the others back to live with a snap and destroyed his hand. Thanos followed the Avengers back and had issued an attack to get the Infinity stone back. The war then started between Thanos and Avengers. In the end, Iron Man snatched the Infinity Stone and snapped his fingers to destroy Thanos but in the end, Iron Man was dead too.
