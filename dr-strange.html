POWERS AND ABILITIES
  • Sorcerer:
    Dr. Strange possesses a vast mastery of the mystic arts; his primary magical patrons are a group of entities known as "the Vishanti."
  • Energy Blasts & Astral Projection:
    Capable of destroying moons and planets and he can release his astral form from his body, instantaneously and at will.
  • Transmutation:
    Dr. Strange has showcased the ability to molecularly alter and convert an object into another using spells, has the ability to magically create illusions and cause others to experience things that aren't real and he can mentally manipulate his environment without physically interacting with it and he can fly using magical energy.
  • Telepathy, Telekinesis & Flight:
    Dr. Strange can psychically communicate and read minds.
  • Protective Shields, Illusions & Intangibility:
    Dr. Strange can shield himself or his allies, he has the ability to become invisible or near-invisible and phase through solid material.
  • Time Manipulation, Teleportation & Dimensional Travel:
    Dr. Strange has can froze slow, and travel time, he can teleport himself and others to any place of his choosing through the use of magic and travel between dimensional planes and access alternate dimensions.
  • Various other spells:
    Dr. Strange has become an expert in the Black Priests's ability to use language to distort reality, becoming even more capable than the Black Priests themselves in a short amount of time.
  • Former Expert Surgeon/Surgical Consultant:
    Strange holds an M.D. in neurosurgery and was once considered one of the foremost experts in his field. Even without magic, Strange retains his medical knowledge.
