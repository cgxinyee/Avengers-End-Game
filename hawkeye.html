POWERS AND ABILITIES
  • Master Archer:
    Barton has trained himself to become a master archer specializing in the use of regular bows, longbows, compound bows, and crossbows with near-perfect accuracy.
  • Peak Human Sight, Condition, Strength, Endurance & Reflexes:
    Barton possesses extraordinary eyesight, bodily functions at the peak-level athlete, physical strength are in peak human levels, exhibits high-level pain and stress tolerance, and reflexes superior to 98% of all normal human beings.
  • Master Marksman:
    Barton is known as the world's most skillful and accurate archer.
  • Expert Acrobat & Master Martial Artist:
    Barton is an athletic person with natural agility and is an excellent martial artist.
  • Expert Tactician:
    As shown in his leadership of the West Coast Avengers and the Thunderbolts, Barton is a highly competent strategist, tactician, and field commander.
  • Cunning Fighter & Weapons Proficiency:
    Hawkeye is shown numerous of times as a smart and clever fighter. Although he is not known to use melee weapons, Barton's incredible reflexes and hand-eye coordination allow him to easily master most weapons.
  • Expert Vehicular operator:
    He's one of the most proficient and daring pilots of the Avengers Quinjets and Sky Cycles.
  • Talented Weapons Designer:
    He has crafted arrows, bows, blades, darts, throwing irons, bolas and axes and many of his own trick arrows.
  • Multilingual:
    Not only is Hawkeye fluent in English, his mother tongue, but also in Italian and in American sign language.
